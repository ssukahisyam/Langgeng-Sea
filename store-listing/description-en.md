## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

**Langgeng Sea — Loyal Trail at Sea.**

Langgeng Sea is a GPS tracking app built specifically for Indonesian
trawl fishers. Use your Android phone to record the trail of your
trawl gear at sea, even with zero signal. No extra GPS hardware, no
subscription, no account required.

---

### 🎣 Core Features

- **🛰️ Offline GPS Tracking**
  Record boat and trawl position without any internet. Your phone's
  built-in GPS chip is enough. Airplane mode? Still works.

- **🎯 Multi-Haul Per Trip**
  One trip out to sea can hold 2–5 hauls. Each is saved separately
  and can be named (e.g. "Haul 1 — Eastern Reef"). Easy to compare.

- **📏 Automatic Calculations**
  Distance, duration, average speed, heading, and swept area are
  computed automatically when you press "Angkat Trawl" (haul up).

- **📓 Digital Log Book**
  Log catch (kg per species, optional), fuel usage, weather, swell,
  crew. Optional fields — fill only what matters to you.

- **🗺️ Offline Maps**
  Download maps (OpenStreetMap + OpenSeaMap nautical overlay) once
  on shore while on WiFi, use them at sea forever.

- **📊 Statistics Dashboard**
  Review catch performance by week / month. Most productive spots,
  average catch per haul, fuel trends, and more.

- **📍 Spot Markers**
  Mark reefs, harbours, or productive fishing spots. Share markers
  with fellow fishers via file.

- **📤 Export / Import**
  GPX format (readable by every GPS tool on the planet) and a native
  `.lsea.json` format for sharing between Langgeng Sea users with
  sender name attribution.

---

### ⛵ When to Use

- **At the harbour, before leaving** — download offline maps, check
  your favourite spot markers.
- **Deploying gear at sea** — tap "Mulai Tebar" (Start Deploy). GPS
  records in the background. You can turn off the screen to save
  battery.
- **Hauling up** — tap "Angkat Trawl" (Haul Up). Summary appears
  immediately: distance, duration, swept area.
- **Next deploy** — tap "Mulai Tebar" again. Langgeng Sea auto-knows
  this is haul #2 of the same trip.
- **Back on shore** — review trip in History. Fill out the log book
  if you want. Export to GPX for archive.

### ✨ Why Langgeng Sea?

**🇮🇩 Built for Indonesian Fishers**
Not a repurposed Western sports tracker. Terminology, workflow, and
button layout follow trawl fisher habits — tebar (deploy), angkat
(haul up), haul, trip.

**🗣️ 100% Bahasa Indonesia**
All buttons, error messages, and help text are in plain Indonesian.
No jargon.

**🔒 Total Privacy**
No account. No server. No analytics. No ads. Your data lives only on
your phone. Full stop. Uninstall = data gone everywhere.

**💰 Free Forever**
No ads, no subscription, no paywalled features. Everything above is
free.

**🔓 Open Source**
Source code public on GitHub. Transparent, auditable, and open to
community contribution.

**🔋 Battery-Efficient**
GPS is only active when you press the button. Efficient foreground
service. A typical 8-hour trip consumes 15-25% battery (Android 8+
with a healthy battery).

---

### 📱 Device Requirements

- Android 8.0 (API 26) or newer.
- GPS chip (practically every Android phone has one).
- At least 200 MB free storage (more if you plan to download large
  offline map areas).
- Not RAM-hungry — runs smoothly from 2 GB up.

---

### ⚠️ What's Not There Yet (Honestly)

Version 1.0 is an MVP. The following aren't in yet and are on the
roadmap:

- **SOS emergency button** (planned v1.1).
- **Device-to-device / cloud sync** (planned v1.2).
- **Geofencing** — alarms when entering restricted zones (planned
  v1.2).
- **iOS version** (planned v2.0 — Android first per primary target
  user base).
- **Premium paid maps** (high-resolution) — to be evaluated in v2.0.
